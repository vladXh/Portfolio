    • Who Am I?

    More Than Just A Title.

    I don’t approach development as just writing code or creating assets—I approach it as solving problems, designing systems, and making sure everything fits together. Whether I’m modeling a prop, architecting a feature, or optimizing a workflow, my goal is always clarity, efficiency, and purpose.

    My background spans full-stack game development, 3D modeling, and game production, shaped by years of collaborative studio work and hands-on experimentation. I value shipping, iteration, and learning from real constraints, because that’s where good ideas turn into solid products.

    Years of full-stack game development and 3D creation have shaped how I work: thoughtfully, efficiently, and with an eye toward production reality. I’ve helped build systems and worlds used by real players, and I focus on delivering work that is deliberate, refined, and built to scale.
